# James Beard Award Nominees & Winners with Chicago Restaurants (Still Open)

All nominees/winners listed below either currently operate or previously operated a restaurant in Chicago. **Only entries where the associated restaurant is still open as of early 2026 are included.**

> **Note:** The James Beard Foundation primarily publishes winners publicly; comprehensive nominee/finalist lists for years prior to ~2022 are not fully archived online. This list includes all winners and confirmed finalists/nominees that could be verified through public sources. Awards were not given in 2020 or 2021 due to the pandemic.
